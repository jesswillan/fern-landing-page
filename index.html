About us

Welcome to Fern!

We are dedicated to sharing the beauty of nature through our curated collection of plants.

Whether you're an experienced plant enthusiast or a beginner, our diverse selection has something for everyone. Our knowledgeable team is here to assist you in finding the perfect plants to create your own green oasis.

    At Fern you will find:

  • Plants

    From calathea to cacti, big to small, needy to low-maintenance, you will find a wide variety of plants to choose from. Ask our knowledgable staff if you're not sure where to start!

  • Tools and decor

    Find all you need to care for your plants, and beautiful pots for them to live in.

  • Gifts

    Need a gift for a fellow plant-lover - or to treat yourself - you can also find plant-themed gifts and prints at Fern.

Repotting service

Customers at Fern have long requested a plant repotting service, and we now provide this at our shop!

Our repotting service includes a new plastic pot of the appropriate size and the potting mix best suited to your plant. We will also advise if your plant needs any special care or attention.
